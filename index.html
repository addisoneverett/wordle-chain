How to play

Guess each word with the guesses you get (Standard uses five rows).

Each word and the next one form a familiar phrase pair—like compound words or common collocations.

Examples

coffee → table → lamp

coffee table, table lamp

ice → cream → soda

ice cream, cream soda

Play words in order. Finish the chain to win—try Endless or Frenzy from the mode menu anytime.
